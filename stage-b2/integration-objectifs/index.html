Onboarding & Roadmap

Integration & Objectives

VIApass Palais des Festivals (Cannes) Network Operations

Arrival Context

Upon arrival, I took over the **deployment plans**, the **supervision** and **ticketing** tools, and toured the **sensitive zones** (press, ticketing, high-density areas). Immediate objective: understand the topology, locate critical points, and align expectations.

Internship Objectives

  • Assimilate the architecture: **segmentation** (VLAN), **high-density Wi-Fi**, **QoS**, and services (DHCP/DNS).
  • Contribute to **supervision**: useful indicators, contextualized thresholds, "event" view.
  • Improve operational **documentation**: "public" diagrams, checklists, playbooks.
  • Participate in **support**: rapid diagnosis, escalation, concise post-mortem.

Expected Deliverables

  • **Unified** maps (homogeneous legends/naming) with a "public" version.
  • **Intervention Playbooks** (diagnosis → escalation → post-mortem).
  • Short reports on **Wi-Fi quality** (trends, priority zones, attention points).
Confidentiality: sensitive information (public IPs, SSIDs, identifiers) are anonymized or reformulated.

Work Method

  • **Double loop**: field (symptoms) ↔ NOC (metrics) for quick decision-making.
  • **Short iterations**: tests on small zones, feedback, threshold/template adjustments.
  • **Living Docs**: versioning, pre-opening checklist, short debrief after closing.

First Actions Completed

  • Reading plans and **zoning** of priority spaces.
  • Rapid audit of **existing diagrams** and proposal for a common template.
  • Field verification: consistent **VLAN/DHCP**, **throughput** tests at key points.

Tools & Stack (Generic)

  • Network supervision (availability, load, RSSI/SNR, contextualized alerting).
  • Structured ticketing (who/what/where/when) with a clear workflow.
  • Field equipment: distribution case, switches (e.g., MikroTik), high-density APs, RJ45 testers.
  • Schematics & documentation templates.

Risks & Mitigations

  • **Alerting noise** → filter by criticality, adapted thresholds, "synthesis view."
  • **Divergent maps** → unique templates, homogeneous naming, "public" version.
  • **Sporadic load** → distribution reinforcement, rebalancing, additional antennas.

Load Ramp-up Plan

  • **Pre-opening**: checklists, calibration measurements, provisional thresholds.
  • **Opening**: targeted monitoring, unique Field ↔ NOC channel, rapid decisions.
  • **Post-day**: 10 min debrief, 3 takeaways → 3 actions for the next day.
