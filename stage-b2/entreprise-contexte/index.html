B2 Internship (2025) · Company & Context

Internship Framework

VIApass — Palais des Festivals (Cannes)

Network Operations High-Density Wi-Fi Monitoring & Support

The Company

VIApass operates and monitors network infrastructures for high-demand sites: events, conventions, and public venues. At the **Palais des Festivals**, the goal is to ensure **reliable**, **secure**, and **readable** connectivity despite significant traffic peaks.

  • Multi-zone LAN / WLAN (public, exhibitors, staff, press)
  • Availability & QoS; segmentation (VLAN), access control
  • Monitoring, local support, on-site coordination

The Environment & Audiences

The Palais aggregates spaces with very different profiles (trade shows, meeting rooms, press areas, halls, booths). Expectations vary: *stability* for ticketing, *throughput* for the press, *simplicity* for the public, and *agility* for the exhibitors.

  • Constraints: short deadlines, mobility of booths, Wi-Fi density, wired/Wi-Fi coexistence.
  • Rhythm: setup phases, public opening, dismantling — very different load profiles.
  • Quality of Service: clear visibility into sensitive areas and rapid incident response.

Internship Context & Objectives

  • Standardize and make **maps** actionable (legends, naming, "public" views).
  • Improve **monitoring**: useful indicators, contextualized thresholds, "event" view.
  • Participate in local **support** (rapid diagnosis, escalation, concise post-mortem).
  • Strengthen **field communication ↔ NOC** to accelerate decision-making.
Confidentiality: sensitive information (public IPs, SSIDs, identifiers, hostnames) are anonymized or masked on published visuals.

Architecture (Very High Level View)

  • Collection/Access: High-density APs, wired distribution (switching), temporary patching.
  • Segmentation: VLAN by usage profile (public/exhibitor/staff/press, etc.).
  • Services: DHCP, DNS, access control; audiovisual interop (addressing, gateway).
  • Monitoring: availability, load, Wi-Fi indicators (RSSI/SNR, median throughput).
  • Support: ticketing, escalation, capitalization (playbooks & checklists).

Roles & Interactions

  • NOC (Network Operations Center): global view, correlation, prioritization.
  • Field: installation, checks, direct interaction with exhibitors/press.
  • Field ↔ NOC Gateway: short messages *who/what/where/when*, photos if useful, clear action plan, confirmation after execution.

Tools (Generic)

  • Network dashboards (load, availability, Wi-Fi, alerting).
  • Ticketing (diagnosis → escalation → resolution → post-mortem).
  • Field tools: VLAN/DHCP test, throughput, port identification (labeling & templates).
  • Schematics & "public" documentation.
